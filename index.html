📖 How to Use EFRIS Connect

1

Open any Sales Invoice or Receipt in Manager.io

The extension automatically reads the invoice details. You will see them above.

2

Check the invoice details are correct

Review the customer name, amount, and line items shown in the Submit tab.

3

Click "Submit to URA EFRIS"

The extension will connect to URA and submit the invoice. This takes about 10–15 seconds.

4

Your FDN will appear

URA returns a Fiscal Document Number (FDN). This is automatically saved to your invoice in Manager.io in the EFRIS FDN custom field.

5

Print your receipt/invoice

The FDN and QR code will appear on your printed document for customer verification.

⚠️ Common Problems

Relay server not running
Open Command Prompt, go to your extension folder, and run: node server.js
"Authentication failed"
Check your TIN, Device Number, and EFRIS password in Settings tab.
"Invoice already submitted"
EFRIS rejects duplicate invoice numbers. This invoice was already submitted successfully.
First time? Start in Sandbox mode
Test with real invoice data in the EFRIS test environment before switching to Production.
