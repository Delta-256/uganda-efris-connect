📖 How to Use
1

Open any Sales Invoice in Manager.io

The extension reads it automatically.

2

Check the details shown above

Confirm customer, amount and items are correct.

3

Click "Submit to URA EFRIS"

Takes 10–20 seconds.

4

FDN is returned and saved

Automatically written to the EFRIS FDN field on your invoice.

5

Print the invoice

FDN and QR code appear on the printed document.
